READ_ME

purpose: compute the shortest path of a grocery trip

user interface: 
1. eight blue rectangle represents the shelves where you can get items
2. the picture above rectangle indicates the grocery category, for example, fresh, frozen, meat, etc...
3. blue dollar sign represents the check out cash register
4. brown door sign represents the entrance to this grocery store
5. No matter what items you buy, your path starts from entrance and end in cash register

Interaction:
Input: grocery list from customer inputOutput: a path that need be followed, with instructions about where to stop to pick up which items

instructions:
1. click on the blue rectangle to indicate the location of grocery items that you need get
2. after choosing location, click on the little button "Done with choosing" below.
3. The program will compute shortest path. Following the pink path will get you all items you need.
